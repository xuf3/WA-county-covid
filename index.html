About this Dashboard

This dashboard diplayed the covid-19 cases information for each counties in Washington State. The data is retrived from Washington State Department of Health. Besides the basic indicators like cases, death, hospitalizations, I also combined the dataset with the population of the county to calculate the mobidity rate for each county as well as a age distribution bar chart. Feel free to check different counties's situation and find the one you live in also various patterns of the covid.
